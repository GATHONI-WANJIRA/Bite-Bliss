North African Cuisine

Influenced by Arab, Berber, and Mediterranean flavors, North African cuisine features dishes like couscous, tagines, falafel, shawarma, and hearty stews like harira.

West African Cuisine

Known for its bold and vibrant flavors, West African cuisine incorporates ingredients like plantains, yams, peanuts, and a variety of spices. Popular dishes include jollof rice, fufu, egusi soup, and grilled suya.

East African Cuisine

East African cuisine reflects a blend of indigenous, Arab, and Indian influences. Staple foods include injera (a sourdough flatbread), ugali (a maize-based porridge), and dishes like nyama choma (grilled meat), samosas, and pilau rice.
